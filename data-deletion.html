Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your personal data associated with our game, please follow the instructions below:

  1. Send an email to free-electron@hotmail.com with the subject "Delete My Data".
  2. In the email, please include your full name, the email address associated with your account, and a brief description requesting the deletion of your data.
  3. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm the deletion via email.

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact us at the same email address.
